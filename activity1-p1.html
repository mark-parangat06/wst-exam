Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients & Method

  • 1 lb shrimp, cleaned
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup lemon soda (Sprite/7Up)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Melt butter in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Put shrimp and cook until color turns orange.
  4. Pour lemon soda, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for 3–5 minutes and serve.
