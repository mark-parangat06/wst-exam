Sauteed Bitter Melon

Ingredients & Method

  • 2 pieces ampalaya, sliced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© garlic and onion.
  2. Add ampalaya and cook until slightly tender.
  3. Pour beaten eggs, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stir gently and cook until eggs are set.
