Scrambled Eggs with Tomatoes

Ingredients & Method

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  1. Beat the eggs with a little salt.
  2. Heat oil, scramble eggs until just set, then remove.
  3. Add more oil, cook tomatoes until softened.
  4. Return eggs, mix gently, season, and serve.
