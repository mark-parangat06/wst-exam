String Beans Adobo

Ingredients & Method

  • 1 bunch sitaw (string beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Heat oil, saut√© garlic and onion.
  2. Add string beans and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour soy sauce, vinegar, and water.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer until sitaw is tender.
